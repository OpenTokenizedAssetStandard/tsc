## How to enroll as a participant
In order to enroll as a participant in the OTAS project, please submit a Pull Request to this [participants](#participants) file listing your name, organization, and date of enrollment, by following the steps described below. 

_:question:Please email community-architects@lfdecentralizedtrust.org if you encounter issues or have questions._

**Step 1. Go to  the [participants](#participants) file** and make sure you are signed into your {GitHub} account

**Step 2. Click on the :pen: icon at the top right of the [participants](#participants) file**
    
[<img src="https://github.com/OpenTokenizedAssetStandard/tsc/blob/main/docs/OTAS_Participants_graphic1.png" width="500">](https://github.com/OpenTokenizedAssetStandard/tsc/blob/main/docs/OTAS_Participants_graphic1.png)
    
**Step 3. Add your name** to the "Participants" section

[<img src="https://github.com/OpenTokenizedAssetStandard/tsc/blob/main/docs/participants.png" width="500">](https://github.com/OpenTokenizedAssetStandard/tsc/blob/main/docs/participants.png)

**Step 4.** **Commit your changes.** Scroll to the bottom of the page and click on the green "Commit changes" button
    
[<img src="https://github.com/OpenTokenizedAssetStandard/tsc/blob/main/docs/commit_changes.png" width="500">](https://github.com/OpenTokenizedAssetStandard/tsc/blob/main/docs/commit_changes.png)

**Step 5. Create a Pull Request** to request that the changes you made to the [participants](#participants) file be added to the "master" branch of the https://{github}.com/finos/{project slug} repository

**Step 5.1. Click on "Pull Request" in the GitHub navigation bar**

[<img src="https://github.com/OpenTokenizedAssetStandard/tsc/blob/main/docs/create-a-pull-request.png" width="500">](https://github.com/OpenTokenizedAssetStandard/tsc/blob/main/docs/create-a-pull-request.png) 
      
**Step 5.2. Click on the green "Compare & pull request" button**

[<img src="https://github.com/OpenTokenizedAssetStandard/tsc/blob/main/docs/compare-and-pull-request.png" width="500">](https://github.com/OpenTokenizedAssetStandard/tsc/blob/main/docs/compare-and-pull-request.png) 
    
**Step 5.3. Choose a title for your Pull Request, add your name and the project name, and then click on "Create Pull Request"**

[<img src="https://github.com/OpenTokenizedAssetStandard/tsc/blob/main/docs/open_pr.png" width="500">](https://github.com/OpenTokenizedAssetStandard/tsc/blob/main/docs/open_pr.png)

_:question:Please email community-architects@lfdecentralizedtrust.org if you encounter issues or have questions._
